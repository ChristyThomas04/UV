Customised UVGI Products


UV-Sentinel is a range of customised UVGI(Ultraviolet Germicidal Irradiation) equipment offered by Hound Electric,available in a wide range of custom sizes, irradiation dosagenand cycle time manufactured according to the requirement.
UVGI is the technology used to dinsinfect materials and surfaces using short-wavelenth ultraviolet light.The Specific range of Ultra-Violet that is used in the disinfection comes between 100nm-280nm wavelengths called the UV-C spectrum.The UV light kills or inactivates microorganisms by destroying nucleic acids and disrupting their DNA,leaving them unable to perform vital cellular functions.It is highly effective between 200nm-300nm.UV disinfection does not leave any residue in the service also.
Our UV-C lamps emit UV radiation at 253.7nm which is highly effective in deactivating microbes.We use advanced materials that ensure high reflection and dispersion for an even spread of the light and provides consistent irradiation during its life time.

Our Upcoming Products


Our Latest upcoming product: UV-S-9000 is used for space disinfection for rooms and surfaces. This method is highly effective as UV-S-9000 is capable of killing about 99.9% if microbes in the surfaces of the room without any contact or using solvents. An added advantage is that the UV-S-9000 kills the microbes in the air also rendering the air free of any suspended organisms that could end up on the surface even after manual cleaning. UV-S-9000 is constructed using high-grade stainless steel and premium materials and switches. It has strategically placed eight UVC lamps that are some of the best that is available in the industry. UV-S-9000 is controlled remotely using a dedicated remote or via an android app, thus the operator can use the equipment and avoid the risk of exposure to high energy UV radiation. Also, to further reduces the risk of accidental UV exposure, UV-S-9000 is fitted with motion sensors that cut off the UV if any motion is detected.
